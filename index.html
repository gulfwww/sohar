Welcome to Sohar Petroleum LTD

Sohar Trading is a prominent company specializing in the trading of fuels and oil-based products in both Kenya and the United Arab Emirates (UAE). With a strong presence in the energy sector, Sohar Trading has established itself as a reliable and trusted partner for businesses and individuals seeking high-quality petroleum products.

About us

In Kenya, Sohar Trading operates as a key player in the country's fuel industry. The company has built a robust supply chain network, ensuring a steady flow of petroleum products to meet the demands of various sectors, including transportation, manufacturing, and agriculture. Sohar Trading takes pride in its efficient logistics and distribution capabilities, allowing it to deliver fuels promptly and reliably across Kenya.

In the United Arab Emirates, Sohar Trading has capitalized on the country's status as a global hub for the oil and gas industry. Leveraging its extensive industry knowledge and expertise, the company serves as a vital link between global suppliers and local consumers, facilitating the smooth import and export of oil-based products. Sohar Trading's operations in the UAE encompass a wide range of fuels, including gasoline, diesel, aviation fuel, and marine fuel.
